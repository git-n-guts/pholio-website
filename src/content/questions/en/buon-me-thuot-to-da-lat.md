The Buon Me Thuot to Da Lat route is one of the Central Highlands' most scenic journeys — crossing mountain passes, coffee plantations, and pine forests before reaching the "City of Eternal Spring."

**Distance and route**

- **Distance**: 200km via QL27
- **Time**: 4-5 hours (bus), 5-6 hours (motorbike with stops)
- **Route**: QL27 southeast through Krong No, Lac Duong, then into Da Lat
- **Elevation**: climbs from 500m (BMT) to 1,500m (Da Lat)

**Bus options** (most common)

| Operator | Type | Price | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| Phuong Trang (Futa) | Sleeper | 300k-400k VND | 6am, 7am, 8pm, 9pm |
| Thanh Binh | Sleeper | 250k-350k VND | 6:30am, 8:30pm |
| Local minibus | Seated | 200k-250k VND | Hourly 5am-4pm |

**Booking**:
- **Online**: futa.vn (Phuong Trang), 12go.asia (aggregator)
- **In person**: Buon Me Thuot bus station, travel agencies
- **Advance**: Not required for day buses; book 1-2 days ahead for sleepers

**Bus types**:
- **Sleeper**: Reclining berths (45-degree angle), blanket provided, water included
- **Seated**: Standard bus seats (less comfortable for 4-5 hour journey)
- **Limousine**: 9-11 seaters, more legroom, higher price (400k-500k VND)

**Motorbike option** (experienced riders only)

**Route details**:
- Follow QL27 southeast from Buon Me Thuot
- Pass through Krong No district (coffee plantations)
- Climb Prenn Pass (15km of switchbacks)
- Descend into Da Lat valley

**Road conditions**:
- QL27: Paved, generally good condition
- Prenn Pass: Steep, multiple blind corners, truck traffic
- Fuel stops: Available every 30-50km

**Recommended stops**:
- **Dran town** (halfway): Coffee break, fuel
- **Prenn Falls viewpoint**: Photo opportunity
- **Datanla Falls** (outskirts of Da Lat): Optional detour

**Safety notes**:
- Check brakes and tires before departure
- Carry rain jacket (weather changes quickly at elevation)
- Avoid night riding (poor visibility, increased risk)
- Not recommended during rainy season (May-October)

**Private car/taxi**

- **Cost**: 1,5M-2,5M VND depending on vehicle type
- **Advantages**: Door-to-door, flexible departure time, AC
- **Booking**: Grab (long-distance option), local taxi companies, hotel concierge
- **Time**: 4 hours (faster than bus, no stops)

**No train or flight options**

- **Train**: No railway connection between Buon Me Thuot and Da Lat (railway closed in 1970s)
- **Flight**: No airport in Buon Me Thuot with commercial service to Da Lat (Lien Khuong Airport)

**Border checkpoints**

- No international borders crossed
- No police checkpoints requiring documentation
- Occasional traffic police speed checks (carry license)

**Best time to travel**

**Dry season (November-April)**:
- Clear skies, good visibility
- Comfortable temperatures (18-25°C in Da Lat)
- Best for motorbike

**Rainy season (May-October)**:
- Afternoon showers common
- Fog on mountain passes
- Bus recommended over motorbike

**Arrival in Da Lat**

**Bus stations**:
- **Da Lat Interprovincial Station**: 3km from center, taxis available
- **Phuong Trang Terminal**: Near Xuan Huong Lake, walkable to some hotels

**Getting to your hotel**:
- **Taxi**: 50k-100k VND from main station
- **Grab**: Available, similar pricing
- **Hotel pickup**: Arrange in advance (some hotels offer free pickup)

**Sample itinerary**

| Time | Activity |
|---|---|
| 6:00am | Depart Buon Me Thuot (Phuong Trang sleeper) |
| 8:30am | Rest stop at Dran (15 minutes) |
| 10:30am | Arrive Da Lat |
| 11:00am | Check in hotel, explore Xuan Huong Lake |
