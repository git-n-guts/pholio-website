Yes, there are **two bus options** from Cam Ranh International Airport to Nha Trang city — an airport shuttle bus and a public city bus. Both are significantly cheaper than taxis but take longer due to multiple stops.

## Airport Shuttle Bus (Recommended for most travelers)

**Price:** 100,000–150,000 VND per person ($4–6 USD)

**Schedule:** Hourly departures from 6:00am to 8:00pm

**Travel time:** 60–70 minutes

**Route:** Cam Ranh Airport → Trần Phú beach road (multiple hotel stops)

**Booking:** Counter inside arrivals hall, or book through your hotel in advance

**How it works:**

1. Buy ticket at the airport shuttle counter (look for "Airport Bus" or "Shuttle Bus" signs)
2. Show your hotel address on Trần Phú street
3. Board the mini-bus (16–29 seats, air-conditioned)
4. Driver drops you at or near your hotel

**Pros:**
- Cheap (1/3 the price of a taxi)
- Hotel drop-off (no need to drag luggage)
- Air-conditioned comfort
- Safe and reliable

**Cons:**
- Slower than taxi (multiple stops)
- Limited schedule (no late-night departures)
- May need to walk 1–2 blocks from your exact hotel

## Public Bus #19 (Budget option)

**Price:** 70,000 VND per person ($2.80 USD)

**Schedule:** 5:30am to 6:00pm (approximately every 30 minutes)

**Travel time:** 70–80 minutes

**Route:** Airport → Highway 1 → North Nha Trang → City Center → Chợ Đầm Market

**Stops in Nha Trang:**
- Northern city entrance (Vĩnh Phước)
- Nguyễn Thiện Thuật street
- Bạch Đằng street
- Chợ Đầm market (final stop)

**How to ride:**

1. Exit the airport terminal
2. Walk 50 meters to the public bus stop (marked with "Bus #19" sign)
3. Pay driver in cash when boarding (have exact change if possible)
4. Tell driver your destination — they'll announce your stop

**Pros:**
- Cheapest option (70k VND)
- Frequent departures
- Authentic local experience
- Runs earlier than shuttle bus (starts 5:30am)

**Cons:**
- No air conditioning (open windows only)
- Can be crowded
- No luggage storage
- Doesn't drop at hotels
- Stops running at 6pm

## When buses don't work

**Arrive after 8pm?** Your only options are:
- Taxi (350k–450k VND)
- Grab (280k–380k VND)
- Pre-arranged hotel pickup (prices vary)

**Heavy luggage?** Take the airport shuttle or taxi. Public bus #19 has no dedicated luggage space.

**Traveling in a group of 3+?** A taxi or Grab becomes cost-effective. Split 400k VND taxi fare 3 ways = 133k VND per person (only slightly more than bus).

## Booking tips

**Airport shuttle:**
- Book at the counter inside arrivals hall
- Have your hotel address written in Vietnamese (show booking confirmation on phone)
- Pay in cash (VND)
- Mini-buses are comfortable but can fill up during peak season

**Public bus #19:**
- Have exact change (70,000 VND)
- Keep your backpack with you (no luggage compartment)
- Press the bell or tell driver when you want to get off
- Not recommended during rainy season (open windows, no AC)

## The verdict

The airport bus is a viable, budget-friendly option for solo travelers and backpackers arriving during daytime hours. The airport shuttle offers the best balance of price and comfort.

**Best for:** Solo travelers, budget backpackers, daytime arrivals
**Skip if:** You arrive after 8pm, have heavy luggage, or are traveling in a group of 3+ (taxi becomes cost-effective)
