Saigon's skyline grew fast — enough glass towers went up in the last decade that several floors above street level now feel like a different city. The rooftop bar business exists because of this: you're paying for the altitude, and whether it's worth it depends on which bar and what you order.

## The premium tier

**Chill Sky Bar** — Hotel des Arts, 76–78 Nguyễn Thị Minh Khai, Level 26, District 3

The most consistently recommended rooftop in Saigon. The view covers the entire District 1 skyline including the Bitexco Tower. The cocktail list is well-executed — their signature mango and passionfruit drinks use local fruit instead of syrup.

Dress code enforced. Reserve a table on weekends.

Hours: 5pm–midnight (closed Tuesdays).

Price: 200,000–320,000 VND per cocktail.

**EON Heli Bar** — Bitexco Financial Tower, Floor 52, 2 Hải Triều, District 1

The highest accessible bar in Saigon. Floor-to-ceiling glass on the helipad floor means unobstructed 360° views. The drinks are average for the price but the elevation makes up for it. Best experienced on a clear weeknight — weekends are crowded and the bar service slows noticeably.

Admission: 200,000 VND (applied as drink credit).

Hours: 5:30pm–midnight.

Price: 200,000–350,000 VND per drink.

**The Summit Rooftop** — Caravelle Hotel, 19–23 Công Trường Lam Sơn, Floor 9, District 1

The most historic option: the Caravelle has been the journalist hotel since the Vietnam War. Floor 9 is modest by Saigon rooftop standards — not the highest — but it overlooks the Opera House and has a quiet, colonial-era atmosphere that the glass tower bars lack. Less crowded, more comfortable.

Hours: 5pm–midnight.

Price: 160,000–280,000 VND per drink.

## The mid-range tier

**Shri Restaurant & Lounge** — Centec Tower, Floor 23, 72–74 Nguyễn Thị Minh Khai, District 3

Combines a rooftop terrace with a full restaurant. Good food, solid cocktails, better value than the premium tier. Visible queues on Friday and Saturday from 7pm onward.

Price: 130,000–230,000 VND per drink.

**The View Rooftop Bar** — 69 Đồng Khởi, District 1

Smaller and lower than the above, but directly on Đồng Khởi — Saigon's main luxury shopping street. Better for watching the street activity than for city skyline views. Relaxed atmosphere, no minimum spend.

Price: 100,000–200,000 VND per drink.

## Practical notes

**Wet season (May–October)**: sudden downpours can close outdoor seating with little warning. Arrive early and sit near indoor cover. Most bars have a rain plan.

**Dry season (November–April)**: the best months for rooftop visits. December–February evenings are the most comfortable.

**Sunset timing**: Saigon is close to the equator. Sunset falls around 6pm year-round, varying only 20–30 minutes between seasons.
