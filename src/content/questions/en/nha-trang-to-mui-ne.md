Traveling from Nha Trang to Mũi Né takes you down Vietnam's southern coast — from a bustling beach city to a laid-back fishing village famous for sand dunes and kite surfing.

## Overview

**Distance:** 180 kilometers

**Travel time:** 3.5–4 hours by bus

**Route:** Coastal Highway 1A south → Phan Rang → Phan Thiết → Mũi Né

**Best for:** Beach lovers, kite surfers, photographers, repeat visitors

**Not for:** Day trip (too far — minimum 2 days/1 night)

## Transportation Options

### Sleeper Bus (Most Popular)

**Companies:** Phương Trang (Futa Bus), Kumho

**Price:** 150,000–200,000 VND one-way ($6–8 USD)

**Schedule:** Departures from 6:00am to 2:00pm

**Frequency:** Every 1–2 hours

**Departure point:**
- Phương Trang: 209 Nguyễn Thiện Thuật, Nha Trang
- Kumho: Nha Trang bus station

**Arrival point:**
- Phương Trang: Mũi Né beach road (Phan Thiết)
- Kumho: Mũi Né station

**Vehicle type:** Sleeper bus with reclining berths

**Features:**
- Air-conditioned
- Reclining sleeper berths (lie flat)
- Onboard water
- Luggage compartment

**Booking:**
- At counter (walk-in)
- Through hotel (small markup)
- Online (12go.asia, Baolau.com)

### Limousine Van (Premium)

**Companies:** Various (book through hotels)

**Price:** 200,000–250,000 VND one-way ($8–10 USD)

**Schedule:** Fixed departures (7am, 10am, 1pm) or on-demand

**Travel time:** 3–3.5 hours (slightly faster, fewer stops)

**Vehicle type:** 9-seater Mercedes Sprinter

**Features:**
- Leather seats (more legroom)
- USB charging ports
- Complimentary water
- Less motion sickness

**Best for:** Travelers who value comfort

### Private Taxi

**Price:** 1,500,000–2,000,000 VND round-trip ($60–80 USD)

**Capacity:** 4 passengers

**Travel time:** 3 hours each way

**Best for:** Families, groups, travelers who value flexibility

**Booking:** Through hotels or Mai Linh/Vinasun

### Train + Taxi (Not Recommended)

**Train:** Nha Trang → Phan Thiết (not direct)

**Price:** 250,000–400,000 VND (train) + 200,000 VND (taxi to Mũi Né)

**Travel time:** 5–6 hours total (train + taxi)

**Why not recommended:**
- No direct train to Mũi Né
- Phan Thiết station is 20 km from Mũi Né
- More expensive and slower than bus

## The Route

**Path:** Nha Trang → Cam Lâm → Ninh Thuận (Phan Rang) → Bình Thuận (Phan Thiết) → Mũi Né

**Scenery:**
- Coastal highway with ocean views
- Fishing villages
- Rice paddies
- Sand dunes (approaching Mũi Né)

**Rest stops:**
- One stop at Phan Rang (halfway point)
- 10–15 minute bathroom break
- Snacks and drinks available

**Road condition:** Paved, two lanes, generally good

## What to Do in Mũi Né

**Red Sand Dunes:**
- Location: 18 km north of Mũi Né center
- Activity: Sandboarding, photos
- Entry: 20,000 VND
- Best time: Late afternoon (cooler, better light)

**White Sand Dunes:**
- Location: 25 km north of Mũi Né
- Activity: Sandboarding, Jeep tours, photos
- Entry: 50,000 VND
- Best time: Sunrise (6–7am) or late afternoon

**Fishing Village:**
- Location: Mũi Né harbor
- Activity: Watch boats return (early morning), buy fresh seafood
- Best time: 5–7am

**Kite Surfing:**
- Location: Mũi Né beach (various spots)
- Season: November to March (best wind)
- Lessons: $50–80 USD per session
- Equipment rental: $20–30 USD per day

**Po Shanu Cham Towers:**
- Location: Outskirts of Phan Thiết
- Activity: Cultural visit, photos
- Entry: 30,000 VND
- History: 8th-century Cham ruins

## Tips for the Journey

**Before departure:**
- Book bus ticket 1 day in advance (peak season)
- Use bathroom before boarding
- Bring water and snacks
- Download offline maps

**Onboard:**
- Keep valuables with you (not in luggage compartment)
- Wear light clothing (AC can be cold)
- Take motion sickness pills if sensitive
- Enjoy coastal views (sit on right side for ocean views)

**Arrival:**
- Confirm return bus schedule when you arrive
- Get hotel recommendation from bus staff (often cheaper)
- Rent motorbike for flexibility (100k–150k VND/day)

## When NOT to Visit Mũi Né

**Avoid:**
- April to October (off-season for kite surfing, less wind)
- Rainy season (Sep–Nov) — dunes less photogenic
- Weekends (crowded with domestic tourists)
- Vietnamese holidays — prices double

## The verdict

Getting from Nha Trang to Mũi Né is easy by bus (3.5–4 hours, 150k–250k VND). However, Mũi Né is not a day trip — plan for at least 2 days/1 night to enjoy the sand dunes and beach.

**Best for:** Kite surfers, photographers, beach lovers, repeat visitors to Nha Trang
**Skip if:** First-time visitor with limited time, not interested in sand dunes
