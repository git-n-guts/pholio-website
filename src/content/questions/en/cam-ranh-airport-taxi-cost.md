A taxi from Cam Ranh International Airport (CXR) to Nha Trang city center costs **350,000–450,000 VND** ($14–18 USD) for a metered car. The trip takes **40–50 minutes** and covers approximately **35 kilometers**.

## Taxi options and prices

### Metered Taxis (Recommended)

**Companies:** Mai Linh (green taxis), Vinasun (white taxis with green/red stripes)

**Price:** 350,000–450,000 VND

**Where to find:** Official taxi stands outside the arrivals hall

**How it works:** Driver turns on the meter when you leave the airport. Final price depends on exact destination in Nha Trang.

**Why use:** Transparent pricing, regulated companies, no negotiation needed.

**Tip:** Walk past the private drivers soliciting inside the terminal. Go straight to the official taxi stand.

### Grab (Cheapest Option)

**Price:** 280,000–380,000 VND

**Where to book:** Grab app (download before arrival or use airport Wi-Fi)

**Pickup:** Arrivals gate curb

**Why use:** Usually 20–30% cheaper than metered taxis. Price is locked in when you book — no surprise charges.

**Note:** Grab drivers wait outside the terminal. You may need to walk to a specific pickup point shown in the app.

### Airport Shuttle Bus

**Price:** 100,000–150,000 VND per person

**Schedule:** Runs hourly from 6am to 8pm

**Stops:** Multiple hotels along Trần Phú beach road

**Travel time:** 60–70 minutes (longer due to multiple stops)

**Booking:** Counter inside arrivals hall or through your hotel

**Best for:** Solo travelers on tight budgets

## Scam alert: unmetered airport taxis

**The scam:**

Private drivers approach you inside the arrivals hall or at the curb. They say things like:

- "Taxi to Nha Trang? Good price, 500k!"
- "No meter, flat rate 600k — better for you"
- "Taxi stand is closed, I take you"

**The reality:**

These drivers charge **500,000–1,000,000 VND** for the same trip — 2–3x the fair price. They target tourists who look confused or tired.

**How to avoid:**

1. **Ignore solicitors** — Don't engage with anyone approaching you inside the terminal
2. **Walk to the taxi stand** — Official counters are clearly marked
3. **Use Grab** — Book from the arrivals gate curb
4. **Know the fair price** — Anything over 450k VND is a ripoff

## Payment tips

**Cash is king:** Most taxi drivers and Grab drivers prefer cash (VND). Have small bills ready.

**USD not accepted:** Drivers won't accept US dollars. Exchange currency at the airport counter or withdraw VND from an ATM.

**Tipping:** Not expected. Round up or leave 10,000–20,000 VND if the driver helps with luggage.

A taxi from Cam Ranh airport to Nha Trang should cost **350,000–450,000 VND**. Use Grab for the best price, or Mai Linh/Vinasun metered taxis for convenience.
