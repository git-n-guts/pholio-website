Cam Ranh airport has a persistent taxi scam targeting arriving tourists. The good news: it's easy to avoid if you know what to watch for.

## The scam

**How it works:**

1. You exit the arrivals hall, tired and disoriented
2. A man in a polo shirt approaches: "Taxi to Nha Trang?"
3. He quotes a "special price": 500,000–800,000 VND
4. If you hesitate, he lowers it: "Okay, 400k — best price for you"
5. You get in the car, no meter, and arrive at your hotel after paying 600k–1M VND

**The reality:**

- The fair price is **350,000–450,000 VND** (metered taxi) or **280,000–380,000 VND** (Grab)
- These scammers charge **2–3x the fair price**
- They have no meter — the price is whatever they say when you arrive
- Arguing at your hotel destination is awkward and potentially unsafe

## Red flags

**Inside the terminal:**
- Anyone saying "Taxi?" or "Nha Trang hotel?"
- People holding handwritten signs with your name (unless you pre-booked)
- Claims like "Taxi stand is closed" or "No more taxis"
- "I know your hotel, very close — 300k only"

**At the curb:**
- Cars without taxi company logos
- Drivers who won't turn on the meter
- "Fixed price" offers that sound reasonable (but are 2x fair price)
- Pressure tactics: "Last taxi today!" or "Price goes up if you wait"

## How to avoid scams

### Rule 1: Ignore solicitors

Don't make eye contact. Don't say "no thanks." Just walk past them. Any engagement — even negative — is an invitation to negotiate.

### Rule 2: Go to the official taxi stand

**Where:** Outside the arrivals hall, follow signs for "Taxi" or "Ground Transport"

**What you'll see:**
- Counters with Mai Linh and Vinasun logos
- Queue of branded taxis
- Staff in uniform

**How it works:**
- Tell the counter staff your hotel address
- They radio the next available driver
- Driver turns on the meter when leaving the airport
- Pay the metered fare (350k–450k VND)

### Rule 3: Use Grab (recommended)

**Why Grab is better:**
- Price is locked in when you book (no surprise charges)
- Route is tracked (no detours)
- Driver details are recorded (license plate, photo, name)
- Usually 20–30% cheaper than metered taxis

**How to use:**
1. Download Grab app before you travel (or use airport Wi-Fi)
2. Connect to Vietnam SIM card or roaming
3. Open app at arrivals curb
4. Select "GrabCar" and confirm pickup location
5. Meet your driver at the designated spot

**Note:** Grab drivers wait outside the terminal. You may need to walk 50–100 meters to the pickup point shown in the app.

## Safe taxi companies

### Mai Linh
- **Color:** Green
- **Phone:** 0258 3 727 727
- **Logo:** "Mai Linh" in white text on green background
- **Meter:** Always on

### Vinasun
- **Color:** White with green and red stripes
- **Phone:** 0258 3 67 67 67
- **Logo:** "Vinasun" on doors
- **Meter:** Always on

## What if you already got scammed?

**At your hotel:**
- Pay the quoted price (don't argue — safety first)
- Note the car's license plate and company logo
- Report to hotel staff (they can help)
- File a complaint with the airport authority (online)
