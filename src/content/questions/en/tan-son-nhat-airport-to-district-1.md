Getting from Tan Son Nhat International Airport (SGN) to District 1 is straightforward. The question is not how — it's which option matches your budget, arrival time, and tolerance for hassle.

## Option 1: Grab (recommended)

**Cost:** 80,000–120,000 VND depending on terminal and demand.
**Time:** 15–25 minutes outside rush hour.
**Availability:** 24/7.

Grab is the Southeast Asian equivalent of Uber. The price is shown before you confirm, the route is tracked on GPS, and payment is handled through the app (cash or card).

**Pickup locations:**

- **Domestic terminal (Terminal 1):** Exit gate 4, walk to the right toward the car park. Look for the Grab sign.
- **International terminal (Terminal 2):** Exit and walk to the designated ride-hailing area on level 1. Follow green signage.

Do not book Grab inside the terminal building — drivers cannot enter and will ask you to walk out.

**Surge pricing:** During rain or rush hour (7–9am, 5–7pm), prices can increase 1.3–1.5x. Wait 15 minutes if you're not in a hurry — surge periods are temporary.

## Option 2: Metered taxi

**Cost:** 130,000–180,000 VND to central District 1.
**Time:** 20–30 minutes.
**Availability:** 24/7.

Only use two companies: **Vinasun** (white with green stripe) and **Mai Linh** (green). Both have official ranks outside arrivals.

**How to find the taxi rank:**

- Exit the arrivals hall.
- Walk straight ahead — do not engage with anyone who approaches you inside the terminal.
- Look for the Vinasun or Mai Linh booth. Staff will direct you to the next available taxi.

**Meter rates (2026):**

- Flag fall (first 1–2 km): 12,000–14,000 VND
- Per km after: 16,000–18,000 VND
- Waiting time (traffic): 160,000 VND/hour

The total should read 130,000–180,000 VND on arrival at Ben Thanh Market or Pham Ngu Lao. Anything above 200,000 VND is a rip-off — dispute before exiting.

## Option 3: Bus 152

**Cost:** 5,000 VND.
**Time:** 60–90 minutes.
**Availability:** 5:30am–6:30pm (every 15–20 minutes).

Bus 152 runs from the airport to Ben Thanh Market. It's cheap, safe, and air-conditioned. But it's slow and not ideal with luggage.

**Where to catch it:**

- Exit the domestic terminal (Terminal 1).
- Walk outside and look for the bus stop on the right.
- The bus is white with blue stripes, marked "152" on the front.

**Route:** Airport → Cong Hoa → Cach Mang Thang Tam → Le Loi → Ben Thanh Market.

**Luggage:** Large bags go in the undercarriage compartment. Keep valuables with you.

**Who should take the bus:** Backpackers with carry-on only, budget travelers, and anyone arriving during daylight who wants to see the city from street level.

## Option 4: Airport limousine

**Cost:** 160,000–200,000 VND.
**Time:** 30–40 minutes (makes multiple stops).
**Availability:** 6am–10pm.

Airport limousines are shared vans that drop passengers at major hotels in District 1. Comfortable but slower than Grab.

**Where to book:** Counters inside both terminals (arrivals level).

**Who should take it:** Travelers staying at major hotels (Continental, Rex, Caravelle, Park Hyatt) who want door-to-door service without the Grab hassle.

## What to avoid

**Unmarked taxis:** Anyone in a regular car offering "taxi, taxi?" inside the terminal. These are illegal and charge 2–3x the meter rate.

**Fixed-price quotes:** A driver quoting 300,000+ VND before you get in is attempting a scam. Walk away.

**Airport exchange counters:** If you need VND for the bus or taxi, exchange only 50–100 USD at the airport (rates are 3–5% worse than city rates). Use ATMs or exchange counters in District 1 for the rest.

## Arrival tips

**SIM card:** Buy a Viettel or Vinaphone SIM at the airport (70,000–100,000 VND with data). You'll need it for Grab.

**Cash:** Withdraw 500,000–1,000,000 VND from an ATM in the terminal (fees: 20,000–40,000 VND for foreign cards). Enough for transport and first meal.

**Grab app:** Download and set up before you land. Link your card or add cash payment method.

## The verdict

**First-time visitors:** Grab. It's the easiest option with clear pricing.

**Budget travelers:** Bus 152 if you arrive before 6pm with carry-on only.

**Late-night arrivals:** Grab or Vinasun/Mai Linh taxi — both operate 24/7.

**Groups of 3+:** Two Grabs or one limousine van — cost per person drops below 50,000 VND.
