You don't need a motorbike to get around Nha Trang — in fact, most travelers do fine without one. The city is compact, the beach road is flat, and ride-hailing apps are cheap and reliable.

## Grab (Recommended)

### Grab Bike (Motorbike Taxi)

**Price:** 15,000–40,000 VND per ride ($0.60–1.60 USD)

**Coverage:** Entire city, including Bãi Dài beaches

**Wait time:** 3–10 minutes in central areas

**Payment:** Cash or linked card

**How it works:**
1. Download Grab app (iOS/Android)
2. Enter pickup and drop-off locations
3. Choose "Grab Bike"
4. See price and driver location
5. Meet driver at pickup point
6. Driver provides helmet
7. Pay at destination

**Best for:** Solo travelers, short trips, beating traffic

**Limitations:**
- Can't carry large bags
- Exposed to weather (rain, sun)
- Must wear helmet (provided)

### Grab Car

**Price:** 40,000–80,000 VND per ride ($1.60–3.20 USD)

**Coverage:** Same as Grab Bike

**Wait time:** 5–15 minutes

**Payment:** Cash or linked card

**Best for:** Couples, families, rainy days, longer trips

**Advantages over Grab Bike:**
- Air conditioning
- No helmet required
- More comfortable
- Can carry luggage

## Public Bus

**Fare:** 7,000 VND per ride (pay driver in cash)

**Main routes:**

### Bus #19 (Airport Route)
- **Route:** Cam Ranh Airport → North Nha Trang → Chợ Đầm
- **Hours:** 5:30am–6:00pm
- **Frequency:** Every 30 minutes
- **Stops:** Airport, highway, Nguyễn Thiện Thuật, Bạch Đằng, chợ Đầm

### Bus #1 (Beach Route)
- **Route:** Vĩnh Phước (North) → Trần Phú → City Center
- **Hours:** 5:00am–7:00pm
- **Frequency:** Every 15–20 minutes
- **Stops:** Beach hotels, Po Nagar Cham Towers, market area

### Bus #2 (City Route)
- **Route:** City Center → West Nha Trang → Residential areas
- **Hours:** 5:30am–6:30pm
- **Frequency:** Every 20–30 minutes

**How to ride:**
1. Wait at bus stop (marked with sign)
2. Raise hand to signal driver
3. Board and pay driver (have exact change)
4. Press bell or tell driver when to stop

**Pros:**
- Cheapest option (7k VND)
- Air-conditioned (most buses)
- Covers main tourist areas

**Cons:**
- Limited routes
- Can be crowded
- No English announcements
- Stops running at 7pm

## Bicycle

**Rental cost:** 50,000–80,000 VND per day ($2–3.20 USD)

**Where to rent:** Hotels, hostels, rental shops on Nguyễn Thiện Thuật

**Best routes:**
- **Trần Phú beach road:** 6 km of dedicated bike lane (mostly)
- **Bạch Đằng:** Parallel street, less traffic
- **Coastal path to Hòn Chồng:** Scenic, moderate traffic

**Pros:**
- Exercise and fresh air
- No fuel costs
- Easy parking
- Eco-friendly

**Cons:**
- Hot during midday (11am–3pm)
- Rainy season is unpleasant
- Limited range (5–10 km max for most people)
- Theft risk (always lock)

**Tip:** Rent in the morning (6–9am) or late afternoon (4–6pm) to avoid heat.

## Walking

**Best areas:**
- **Trần Phú beachfront:** Wide sidewalks, pedestrian-friendly
- **Nguyễn Thiện Thuật:** Backpacker area, everything within 10 minutes
- **Bạch Đằng:** Parallel to beach, local restaurants and shops

**Distances:**
- North end (Vĩnh Phước) to south end (cable car station): 6 km
- Most hotels to beach: 1–5 minutes
- Most hotels to restaurants: 2–10 minutes

**Pros:**
- Free
- Best way to explore
- No parking hassles
- Good exercise

**Cons:**
- Hot during midday
- Sidewalks sometimes blocked by vendors
- Limited range
- Not practical for rainy days

**Tip:** Wear sandals or breathable shoes — flip-flops are acceptable everywhere.

## Taxis (Alternative to Grab)

**Companies:** Mai Linh (0258 3 727 727), Vinasun (0258 3 67 67 67)

**Price:** Similar to Grab Car (40k–80k VND for short trips)

**How to use:**
- Call for pickup
- Find at taxi stands (beach road, market area)
- Flag down on street (if light is on, taxi is available)

**When to use:**
- Grab surge pricing (rare in Nha Trang)
- No Grab drivers nearby
- You prefer metered taxis

## Sample itineraries without motorbike

### Beach day (no transport cost)

**Morning:** Walk from hotel to beach (2 min)
**Lunch:** Walk to nearby restaurant (5 min)
**Afternoon:** Walk back to hotel for nap (5 min)
**Dinner:** Grab Bike to local restaurant (20k VND)
**Total:** 20,000 VND

### City exploration day

**Morning:** Bus to Po Nagar Cham Towers (7k VND)
**Midday:** Grab Bike to chợ Đầm market (25k VND)
**Lunch:** Eat at market (walking distance)
**Afternoon:** Walk along Trần Phú (free)
**Dinner:** Grab Car back to hotel (50k VND)
**Total:** 82,000 VND

### Bãi Dài beach day

**Morning:** Grab Car to Bãi Dài (70k VND)
**Day:** Beach time (no transport)
**Lunch:** Beachfront restaurant (walking)
**Evening:** Grab Car back (70k VND)
**Total:** 140,000 VND (split between 2 = 70k each)

## The verdict

Getting around Nha Trang without a motorbike is easy and affordable. Use Grab for most trips, walk for short distances, and try the public bus for budget travel.

**Best for:** All travelers — motorbike rental is optional, not necessary
**Tip:** Download Grab app before you arrive — it's the most convenient option
