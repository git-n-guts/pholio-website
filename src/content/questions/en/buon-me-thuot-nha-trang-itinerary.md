Buôn Ma Thuột and Nha Trang make a natural pairing — highlands culture meets coastal relaxation, just 200km apart. This itinerary balances coffee plantations with island hopping.

**Why combine them**

**Contrast**:
- BMT: Inland highlands, coffee culture, ethnic minorities, waterfalls
- Nha Trang: Coastal city, beaches, islands, seafood, diving

**Complementary**:
- BMT: Active exploration (motorbike, hiking, village visits)
- Nha Trang: Relaxation (beach time, spa, slow island days)

**Logistics**:
- Direct bus connection (no backtracking)
- Short travel time (4-5 hours)
- Affordable (200k-350k VND)

**Recommended itinerary lengths**

**5 days (minimum)**:
- Days 1-3: Buôn Ma Thuột
- Days 4-5: Nha Trang
- Travel: BMT → Nha Trang on Day 3 afternoon or Day 4 morning

**6-7 days (ideal)**:
- Days 1-3: Buôn Ma Thuột
- Days 4-7: Nha Trang
- Travel: BMT → Nha Trang on Day 3 or 4
- More relaxed pace, extra beach time

**8-10 days (with Da Lat)**:
- Days 1-3: Buôn Ma Thuột
- Days 4-5: Đà Lạt (stopover)
- Days 6-10: Nha Trang
- Travel: BMT → Da Lat → Nha Trang (split over 2 days)

**Day-by-day: 6-day itinerary**

**Day 1: Arrive Buôn Ma Thuột**
- Morning: Arrive (fly from HCMC/Hanoi or bus from coast)
- Afternoon: Check in, Dak Lak Museum (195 Lê Duẩn, 50k VND)
- Evening: Nguyen Hue Walking Street (if Fri-Sun), local dinner
- Sleep: BMT

**Day 2: BMT highlights**
- Morning: Buon Akô Dhông cultural village (5km, Grab 50k VND)
- Lunch: Local com tam or bun rieu near market
- Afternoon: City Park, World Coffee Museum
- Evening: Coffee tasting at local cafe (Trung Nguyen, Legend)
- Sleep: BMT

**Day 3: Lak Lake day trip**
- Morning: Depart 8am for Lak Lake (55km, 1.5 hours)
- Midday: Boat ride (100k VND), Jun Village visit
- Lunch: Lakeside homestay
- Afternoon: Return to BMT, pack
- Evening: Depart for Nha Trang (sleeper bus 8pm) OR stay BMT
- Sleep: BMT or bus

**Day 4: Travel to Nha Trang**
- Morning: Bus BMT → Nha Trang (6am or 8am departure)
- Midday: Arrive Nha Trang (11am-1pm), check in
- Afternoon: Beach time (free, relax after travel)
- Evening: Seafood dinner on Tran Phu Street
- Sleep: Nha Trang

**Day 5: Nha Trang islands**
- Morning: Island hopping tour (4-island, 300k-500k VND)
- Afternoon: Snorkeling, beach lunch
- Evening: Return 4pm, night market
- Sleep: Nha Trang

**Day 6: Departure or extend**
- Morning: Mud bath experience (I-Resort, 200k VND)
- Afternoon: Last beach time, souvenir shopping
- Evening: Depart or extend stay

**Day-by-day: 8-day itinerary (with Da Lat)**

**Days 1-3**: Buôn Ma Thuột (same as above)

**Day 4: BMT → Đà Lạt**
- Morning: Bus BMT → Da Lat (6am, 4-5 hours)
- Afternoon: Arrive Da Lat, check in, Xuan Huong Lake walk
- Evening: Da Lat night market, hot pot dinner
- Sleep: Da Lat

**Day 5: Đà Lạt exploration**
- Morning: Crazy House, Da Lat Market
- Afternoon: Truc Lam Pagoda (cable car, valley views)
- Evening: Cafe hopping (Da Lat has Vietnam's best cafe scene)
- Sleep: Da Lat

**Day 6: Da Lat → Nha Trang**
- Morning: Bus Da Lat → Nha Trang (8am, 3-4 hours)
- Afternoon: Arrive Nha Trang, beach time
- Evening: Seafood dinner
- Sleep: Nha Trang

**Days 7-8: Nha Trang**
- Day 7: Island hopping or diving
- Day 8: Mud baths, departure

**Transportation: BMT ↔ Nha Trang**

**Direct bus** (recommended):

| Operator | Type | Price | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| Phuong Trang | Sleeper | 250k-350k VND | 6am, 8am, 8pm |
| Hoang Long | Seated | 200k-300k VND | Hourly 5am-5pm |
| Local minibus | Seated | 200k-250k VND | When full |

**Booking**:
- Online: futa.vn, 12go.asia
- In person: BMT bus station, travel agencies
- Advance: Not required (walk-up fine except holidays)

**Route highlights**:
- QL27 east through mountains
- Coffee plantations → coastal plains
- Ocean view on final approach to Nha Trang

**Via Da Lat** (scenic detour):
- BMT → Da Lat: 200km, 4-5 hours
- Da Lat → Nha Trang: 140km, 3-4 hours
- Total: 7-9 hours (split over 2-3 days)
- Worth it: If you have 8+ days total

**Best time for this itinerary**

**Ideal**: November-April (dry season for both cities)
- BMT: Dry, comfortable (20-28°C)
- Nha Trang: Dry, calm seas (good for islands/diving)

**Acceptable**: May-August
- BMT: Afternoon showers (plan mornings)
- Nha Trang: Dry, hot (peak beach season)

**Avoid**: September-October
- BMT: Heavy rain (slippery roads)
- Nha Trang: Rainy season, rough seas (island tours cancelled)

**Where to stay**

**Buôn Ma Thuột**:
- **Budget**: Homestays near market (200k-300k VND)
- **Mid-range**: City center hotels (800k-1,5M VND)
- **Luxury**: Muong Thanh, Damsan (2M-3M VND)

**Nha Trang**:
- **Budget**: Backpacker hostels near beach (150k-300k VND)
- **Mid-range**: Beachfront hotels (1M-2M VND)
- **Luxury**: Intercontinental, Vinpearl (4M-10M+ VND)

**Extension options**

**Add Đà Lạt** (2-3 days):
- Between BMT and Nha Trang
- Cool climate, cafes, countryside
- Adds: 7-9 hours travel (split over 2 days)

**Add HCMC** (2-3 days):
- After Nha Trang (train 4 hours, bus 8 hours)
- Urban contrast, history, food scene
- Adds: 4-8 hours travel

**Add Hoi An** (2-3 days):
- Fly Nha Trang → Da Nang (1 hour)
- Ancient town, beaches, tailors
- Adds: Flight cost (1M-2M VND)

**The verdict**

**Worth combining if**:
- You have 5-7 days minimum
- You want highlands + coast contrast
- You're traveling Central Highlands region
- You value cultural + beach experiences

**Skip combination if**:
- You have under 5 days (focus on one destination)
- You're purely beach-focused (skip BMT)
- You're purely culture-focused (skip Nha Trang)
- You're in rush (don't appreciate either properly)
