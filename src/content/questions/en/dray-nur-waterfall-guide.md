Dray Nur is the most accessible and impressive waterfall near Buon Me Thuot. This guide covers everything you need for a day trip.

**Route from Buon Me Thuot**

1. Start from city center
2. Follow QL14 south (signposted toward Dak Nong)
3. After 23km, turn left at H'Drung junction (signs to Dray Nur)
4. Follow local road 3km to entrance
5. Park at designated area, walk 10 minutes to viewpoint

Total: 26km, 40 minutes.

**Transport options**

| Method | Cost | Time | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Motorbike rental | 120k-180k VND/day | 40 min | Most flexible, parking 10k-20k |
| Grab | 200k-300k VND each way | 30 min | May wait 10-20 min for pickup |
| Guesthouse tour | 300k-500k VND | 40 min | Includes entrance, half-day |

**At the falls**

**Entrance fee**: 30,000-50,000 VND (verify 2026)
**Combined ticket** (Dray Nur + Dray Sap): 50,000-80,000 VND

**Walking path**:
- Entrance to viewpoint: 10 minutes, paved, easy
- Viewpoint to pool level: 15 minutes, stairs, steep
- Total walking: 25-30 minutes round trip

**Swimming**:
- Designated areas below the falls
- Water cool and clear in dry season
- Changing rooms: 10,000 VND
- Bring swimwear and towel

**Food**:
- Food stalls at entrance area
- Com lam (bamboo rice): 30,000-50,000 VND
- Grilled corn: 10,000-20,000 VND
- Drinks: 15,000-30,000 VND
- No food at the falls themselves

**Suggested itinerary**

| Time | Activity |
|---|---|
| 8:00am | Depart Buon Me Thuot |
| 8:40am | Arrive Dray Nur, park |
| 9:00am | Walk to viewpoint, photos |
| 9:30am | Down to pool level, swim |
| 10:30am | Walk back, snack at entrance |
| 11:00am | Optional: visit Dray Sap (1km) |
| 12:00pm | Return to Buon Me Thuot |

**Safety notes**

- Heed warning signs about swimming areas
- Don't swim if water is brown or rising (rainy season)
- Watch for slippery rocks near the falls
- Keep valuables in waterproof bag
- Trails can be slippery after rain

**Combining with other attractions**

Dray Nur works well combined with:
- Dray Sap waterfall (1km upstream)
- Lak Lake (30km further south)
- Buon Don elephant village (on the way back)

Full day trip: Buon Me Thuot → Dray Nur → Dray Sap → Lak Lake → Buon Don → back to city.
