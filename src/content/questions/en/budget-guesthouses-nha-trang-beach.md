Yes, Nha Trang has excellent budget guesthouses near the beach — private rooms from 250,000–500,000 VND ($10–20 USD) that are clean, safe, and well-located.

## Where to Find Budget Guesthouses

### Bạch Đằng Street (Best Value)

**Location:** Parallel to Trần Phú beach (5-min walk to sand)

**Price range:** 300,000–500,000 VND/night ($12–20 USD)

**Characteristics:**
- Local restaurants everywhere
- Quieter than beachfront
- Easy access to everything
- Less touristy atmosphere

**What you get:**
- Private room with AC
- Private bathroom with hot shower
- TV, mini-fridge
- Wi-Fi (usually strong)
- Sometimes breakfast included

**Example guesthouses:**
- House 100+ series on Bạch Đằng
- Family-run establishments

### Side Streets Off Trần Phú (Hidden Gems)

**Locations:**
- Hẻm (alley) 100–150 Trần Phú
- Side streets between Trần Phú and Bạch Đằng

**Price range:** 350,000–550,000 VND/night ($14–22 USD)

**Characteristics:**
- 1–2 min walk to beach
- Quieter than main road
- Local neighborhood feel
- Harder to find without GPS

**What you get:**
- Same as Bạch Đằng
- Closer to beach
- Sometimes ocean glimpses from upper floors

### Nguyễn Thiện Thuật (Backpacker Central)

**Location:** 2 blocks inland from beach

**Price range:** 250,000–450,000 VND/night ($10–18 USD)

**Characteristics:**
- Backpacker street
- Noisy at night
- Cheap food everywhere
- Easy to meet travelers

**What you get:**
- Basic private rooms
- AC, fan options
- Shared or private bathrooms
- Social atmosphere

**Best for:** Solo travelers, under-35, party seekers

## What to Expect at This Price

### Room Features (250k–350k VND / $10–14)

**Basics:**
- Double bed or twin beds
- AC or fan (confirm which before booking)
- Private or shared bathroom
- Hot shower (usually 6pm–10pm only)
- TV (Vietnamese channels, sometimes CNN/BBC)

**Bathroom:**
- Western toilet or squat toilet
- Bucket shower or basic shower head
- Soap provided (shampoo rarely)
- Towel: sometimes provided, sometimes rent (5k VND)

**Room condition:**
- Basic decor (painted walls, tile floor)
- Window (may face interior shaft)
- Desk or small table
- Clothes rack or basic wardrobe

### Room Features (400k–500k VND / $16–20)

**Upgrades:**
- Larger room
- Better decor (wood furniture, curtains)
- AC (not just fan)
- Private bathroom guaranteed
- Hot shower 24/7 (solar or electric heater)

**Extras:**
- Mini-fridge
- Kettle for tea/coffee
- Complimentary water bottles
- Daily cleaning
- Sometimes breakfast (phở, bread, coffee)

**Building:**
- Elevator (not guaranteed, ask)
- 24/7 reception
- Luggage storage
- Motorbike rental arranged

## How to Book

### Online (Booking.com, Agoda)

**Advantages:**
- See photos and reviews
- Free cancellation (usually)
- Pay at property (often)
- English interface

**Disadvantages:**
- Prices 10–20% higher than walk-in
- Service fees included
- Can't inspect room first

**Best for:**
- High season (Dec–Feb, Jun–Aug)
- First-time visitors
- Short stays (1–3 nights)

### Walk-In (Recommended for Low Season)

**Advantages:**
- Negotiate price (10–20% off)
- Inspect room before committing
- No booking fees
- Can request specific room

**Disadvantages:**
- Need to carry luggage while searching
- Risk of no availability (high season)
- Language barrier possible

**Best for:**
- Low season (Sep–Nov)
- Long stays (5+ nights)
- Experienced travelers

**How to negotiate:**
1. Ask to see room first
2. Check condition (AC, hot water, noise)
3. Mention length of stay ("I stay 5 nights")
4. Offer cash payment
5. Ask: "Giá tốt nhất?" (best price?)

## Sample Guesthouses

### Budget Tier (250k–350k VND)

**Typical features:**
- 10–12 sqm room
- Fan or basic AC
- Shared bathroom (sometimes)
- No elevator (1st–2nd floor only)
- Basic furniture

**Best for:** Solo travelers, very budget-conscious

### Mid Tier (400k–500k VND)

**Typical features:**
- 15–20 sqm room
- AC guaranteed
- Private bathroom
- Possibly elevator
- Mini-fridge, kettle

**Best for:** Couples, comfort-seeking solo travelers

## Booking Tips

### Questions to Ask

1. "Máy lạnh không?" (Does it have AC?)
2. "Nước nóng 24 giờ?" (Hot water 24 hours?)
3. "Có thang máy?" (Is there an elevator?)
4. "Giá bao nhiêu một đêm?" (Price per night?)
5. "Ở lâu có giảm giá?" (Discount for long stay?)

### Red Flags

- No hot water (unless you don't care)
- Street-level room (noisy, less safe)
- No window (dark, stuffy)
- Mold in bathroom (humidity problem)
- Broken AC (test before accepting)
- Noise from street or neighboring rooms

### Green Flags

- Recent renovation (fresh paint, new furniture)
- High floor (quieter, better view)
- Elevator building (easier with luggage)
- 24/7 reception (flexible check-in)
- Good Wi-Fi (test on your phone)
- Daily cleaning included
