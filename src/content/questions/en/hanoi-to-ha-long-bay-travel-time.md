The distance from Hanoi to Hạ Long Bay's main pier at Bãi Cháy is approximately 160km. The construction of the Hạ Long–Hải Phòng Expressway has reduced the journey from what used to be a 4–5 hour slog on a rural highway to a consistently 2.5–3.5 hour drive.

## By transport type

| Transport | Door-to-pier time | Cost | Notes |
|-----------|------------------|------|-------|
| Tourist shuttle (group tour) | 3–3.5 hours | Included in tour | Hotel pickup available |
| Private minivan | 2.5–3 hours | 350,000–600,000 VND/person | Faster, more direct |
| Self-drive / rental car | 2.5–3 hours | Fuel + tolls ~200,000 VND | Requires Vietnamese driving license |
| Seaplane | 45 min + airport transfers | 2,000,000–3,500,000 VND | Premium option |

## What adds time in practice

**Hotel pickup routing**: group tour buses stop at 5–10 hotels before leaving Hanoi. If your hotel is the second pickup stop, you'll arrive earlier than the group but wait in the bus for 30–45 minutes while the rest board.

**Pier loading**: the main Bãi Cháy pier processes hundreds of boats daily. During peak hours (8:30–10am), waiting for your boat assignment and boarding adds 30–60 minutes after arrival at the pier.

**Budget for**: departure time + 3.5 hours travel + 30 minutes pier loading = 4 hours from your hotel to being on the water.

## For day trips

A standard day trip from Hanoi departs at 7–8am and returns by 7–8pm. Of a 12-hour day, approximately 6–7 hours are spent in transit (combined both ways plus pier time). You get 4–5 hours on the water. This is the honest arithmetic of a Hạ Long Bay day trip — useful for understanding [whether the day trip or overnight cruise](/questions/ha-long-bay-day-trip-vs-overnight/) is the better choice for your schedule.
