Street food is how Buon Me Thuot eats. The city doesn't have a dense network of restaurants — it has stalls, carts, and family operations that serve breakfast, lunch, and disappear by afternoon.

**Breakfast stalls (5am-9am)**

Look for:
- Plastic stools on sidewalks
- Steam rising from large pots
- Families eating together before school/work

What to order:
- **Pho bo**: Beef noodle soup, 40,000-60,000 VND
- **Banh mi**: Baguette sandwiches, 20,000-35,000 VND
- **Xoi**: Sticky rice with toppings, 15,000-30,000 VND
- **Banh cuon**: Steamed rice rolls, 30,000-50,000 VND

Where: Residential alleys, near schools, market entrances.

**Central market perimeter (all day)**

The market (Cho Buon Ma Thuot) has food stalls circling the building:
- **Com lam**: Rice in bamboo tubes, 30,000-50,000 VND
- **Thit nuong**: Grilled pork skewers, 10,000-20,000 VND/skewer
- **Trai cay**: Fresh fruit (mango, dragon fruit, rambutan), 20,000-40,000 VND
- **Nuoc mia**: Sugarcane juice, 10,000-15,000 VND

**Evening Le Duan Boulevard (6pm-10pm)**

After sunset, grills come out:
- **Bo nuong**: Grilled beef skewers, 15,000-25,000 VND/skewer
- **Heo nuong**: Grilled pork, 20,000-30,000 VND/skewer
- **Ca nuong**: Grilled fish, 50,000-80,000 VND
- **Bia hoi**: Fresh beer, 10,000-15,000 VND/glass

**Late-night Nguyen Tat Thanh (10pm+)**

For night owls:
- **Pho**: Late-night bowls, 50,000-70,000 VND
- **Hu tieu**: Noodle soup, 40,000-60,000 VND
- **Che**: Sweet dessert soup, 15,000-25,000 VND

**Hygiene tips**

- Eat where locals eat — turnover keeps ingredients fresh
- Watch food being cooked (avoid pre-cooked items sitting out)
- Peel fruit yourself or buy pre-peeled from busy vendors
- Bring hand sanitizer
- Bottled water only (check seal is intact)

**Dietary notes**

- Vegetarian: Look for "chay" signage (Buddhist vegetarian)
- Allergies: Point and shake head "no" — vendors understand
- Halal: Very limited — ask at the mosque for recommendations

**Budget per day**

- Breakfast: 20,000-40,000 VND
- Lunch: 40,000-70,000 VND
- Dinner: 50,000-100,000 VND
- Snacks/drinks: 20,000-40,000 VND
- **Total: 130,000-250,000 VND/day** for full street food diet
