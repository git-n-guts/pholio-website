Buon Me Thuot is a mid-sized highland city — larger than a town but smaller than major cities like Hanoi or HCMC. Getting around is straightforward with several transport options.

**Motorbike rental** (most flexible)

**Pricing**:
- **Semi-automatic**: 120k-150k VND/day
- **Automatic (Honda Airblade, Yamaha NVX)**: 150k-180k VND/day
- **Weekly rate**: 800k-1M VND (discount for 7+ days)

**Where to rent**:
- **Hotels/guesthouses**: Most arrange for guests (sometimes free for long stays)
- **Rental shops**: Scattered around city (ask at hotel for nearest)
- **GrabBike drivers**: Some offer daily rental (negotiate rate)

**Requirements**:
- **License**: Vietnamese license required by law; International Driving Permit with motorcycle endorsement recommended
- **Deposit**: 1-2M VND cash or passport (avoid leaving passport)
- **Photos**: Document existing damage before riding

**Fuel**:
- **Stations**: Petrolimex, PV Oil throughout city
- **Price**: ~23,000 VND/liter (RON 95)
- **Range**: Most bikes 100-150km per tank

**Grab/GrabBike** (convenient, app-based)

**Availability**:
- **Hours**: 6am-10pm (limited late night)
- **Coverage**: City center and main neighborhoods
- **Wait time**: 3-10 minutes typical

**Pricing** (approximate):
- **Short trips** (1-3km): 50k-70k VND (GrabBike), 70k-100k VND (GrabCar)
- **Medium trips** (3-7km): 70k-100k VND (GrabBike), 100k-150k VND (GrabCar)
- **Long trips** (7km+): 100k-150k VND (GrabBike), 150k-250k VND (GrabCar)

**Payment**:
- **Cash**: Most common, pay driver directly
- **GrabPay**: Link credit card in app (convenient, not widely used)

**Tips**:
- Set pickup location accurately (use map pin)
- Wait at visible location (drivers can't call easily)
- Have small bills for payment (drivers may not have change)

**Motorbike taxi (xe om)** (informal, quick)

**Where to find**:
- Street corners throughout city
- Outside markets, bus stations, hotels
- Drivers wear vests or stand near bikes

**Pricing**:
- **Short trips** (1-2km): 30k-50k VND
- **Medium trips** (2-5km): 50k-80k VND
- **Negotiation**: Agree on price before getting on

**Pros**:
- No app needed
- Available late night (when Grab is scarce)
- Drivers know city well

**Cons**:
- No fixed pricing (tourists sometimes quoted higher)
- No insurance coverage
- Language barrier (Vietnamese only)

**Traditional taxi** (metered, air-conditioned)

**Major companies**:
- **Mai Linh**: 0262.389.89.89 (green taxis)
- **Thang Loi**: 0262.385.85.85 (white taxis)
- **Local taxis**: Various smaller companies

**Pricing**:
- **Flag fall**: 15k-20k VND (first 1-2km)
- **Per km**: 12k-16k VND
- **Typical trip**: 50k-150k VND depending on distance

**Payment**:
- Cash only (mostly)
- Request receipt if needed (for expense claims)

**Bicycle** (eco-friendly, limited)

**Availability**:
- **Hotel bikes**: Some hotels provide free for guests
- **Rental shops**: Limited, ask at hotel
- **Purchase**: Cheap bikes available at markets (1-2M VND)

**Best for**:
- City center exploration
- Park visits
- Short distances (under 3km)

**Limitations**:
- No dedicated bike lanes
- Hot during midday (especially Apr-Aug)
- Rainy season: roads slippery

**Walking** (feasible for central area)

**Walkable areas**:
- **Nguyen Hue Walking Street**: Evening pedestrian zone (Fri-Sun)
- **City Park**: Central green space
- **Museum area**: Around 195 Le Duan
- **Market area**: Cho Buon Ma Thuot

**Walking distances** (from city center):
- **Buon Akô Dhông village**: 5km (not walkable, take Grab)
- **Dak Lak Museum**: 1km (walkable, 15 minutes)
- **City Park**: 0.5km (walkable, 5 minutes)
- **Nguyen Hue Walking Street**: 0.3km (walkable, 3 minutes)

**Safety tips**:
- Sidewalks can be uneven or blocked
- Cross streets carefully (motorbikes don't always yield)
- Keep bags secure (snatch theft rare but possible)

**Parking**

- **Motorbike parking**: Free at most attractions, markets
- **Car parking**: Limited in city center; use hotel parking
- **Attendants**: Some areas have paid parking (5k-10k VND)
