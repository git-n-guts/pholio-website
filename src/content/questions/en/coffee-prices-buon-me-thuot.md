Coffee prices in Buon Me Thuot vary wildly depending on where you buy and what you're buying. This guide breaks down the ranges so you know what to expect.

**Drinking coffee (per ly)**

| Venue type | Price range | What you get |
|---|---|---|
| Roadside stall | 15,000-25,000 VND | 100% Robusta, phin filter, plastic stool |
| Local cafe (ceiling fans) | 25,000-45,000 VND | Robusta or blend, AC, indoor seating |
| Trung Nguyen flagship | 45,000-120,000 VND | Premium blends, fancy presentation |
| Hotel cafe | 50,000-100,000 VND | Tourist pricing, inconsistent quality |
| Weasel coffee (caged) | 100,000-200,000 VND | Unethical, often fake |
| Weasel coffee (wild) | 300,000+ VND/ly | Extremely rare, verify source |

**Buying coffee beans (per kg)**

| Type | Price range | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Robusta green (nhan song) | 50,000-80,000 VND | Roast at home, freshest |
| Robusta roasted | 100,000-200,000 VND | Ready to brew |
| Blend (70% R, 30% A) | 120,000-250,000 VND | Middle ground |
| Arabica (imported Da Lat) | 200,000-400,000 VND | For black coffee lovers |
| Honey-processed | 250,000-500,000 VND | Specialty, limited availability |
| Wild civet (100g) | 3,000,000+ VND | Per 100g, not per kg |

**Where to buy**

**Central market (Cho Buon Ma Thuot)**: Best prices, widest selection. Farmers sell directly. Green beans 50,000-80,000 VND/kg. Roasted 100,000-200,000 VND/kg.

**Roadside stalls**: Some sell bags of their house blend. Ask: "Ban co ban ca phe mang ve khong?" (Do you sell coffee to take home?)

**Trung Nguyen flagship**: Premium pricing, consistent quality, gift packaging. Good for gifts, not daily drinking.

**Plantations**: During harvest, farm-gate prices are lowest. Green beans 40,000-60,000 VND/kg if you buy direct.

**Airport gift shops**: Avoid. Prices are 200-300% higher than central market for the same or lower quality.

**Price comparison: Buon Me Thuot vs elsewhere**

| Item | Buon Me Thuot | Saigon/Hanoi | Airport |
|---|---|---|---|
| Roadside coffee | 15k-25k VND | 20k-35k VND | 50k-80k VND |
| Robusta 1kg | 100k-200k VND | 150k-300k VND | 400k-600k VND |
| Arabica 1kg | 200k-400k VND | 250k-500k VND | 600k-1M VND |

**Tips for buying**

- Taste before buying large quantities
- Ask for roast date — fresher is better (within 2-4 weeks)
- Green beans stay fresh 6-12 months; roasted beans peak at 2-4 weeks
- Bargain at the central market (start at 70% of asking price)
- Avoid pre-packaged gift bags — markup is high, quality unknown
