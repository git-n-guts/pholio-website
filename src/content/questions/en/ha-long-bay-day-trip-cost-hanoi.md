The price of a Hạ Long Bay day trip from Hanoi is well-defined by the tour market. The cheapest options are genuine and deliver the core experience; the expensive options add boat quality and group size reduction. Here's the full breakdown.

## Price tiers (2026)

### Budget group tours: 700,000–1,200,000 VND

These tours run on large traditional junk boats with 20–40 passengers. They operate daily from Hanoi's Old Quarter area pickup points, drive 3–3.5 hours to the Bãi Cháy pier, spend 4–5 hours on the water visiting 1–2 caves and a floating village, have lunch on the boat, and return to Hanoi by 8–9pm.

What you get for this price: the bay, the karst formations, one cave, lunch, transport. What you sacrifice: group size, boat quality, time on the water.

**Booking channels**: Hanoi tour agencies on Hàng Bè, Hàng Bạc, and the streets around the Old Quarter. Online via Viator or GetYourGuide for confirmed pricing, though these carry a platform markup. Street-level direct booking saves 100,000–200,000 VND on the same tour.

### Mid-range small-group tours: 1,200,000–1,800,000 VND

Groups of 8–15 people on a better-quality boat. More deck space, a proper dining area, and a guide who has time to explain what you're looking at rather than just directing traffic. Includes kayaking in some packages.

### Premium day tours: 2,000,000–3,000,000+ VND

Private or semi-private boats (4–8 people), a full itinerary including swimming stops, kayaking, and a superior lunch. These close the gap with the overnight experience in terms of on-water time but still don't solve the sunrise/sunset problem.

## The overnight comparison

A budget 2-day/1-night cruise from Hanoi typically costs 1,500,000–2,500,000 VND per person including cabin, all meals, a full day on the water, kayaking, and sunrise on the bay. The marginal cost over a day trip is 500,000–1,000,000 VND for a dramatically different experience.

If you're choosing between a premium day tour at 2,500,000 VND and a budget overnight at 2,000,000 VND — the overnight is better value for what it delivers.

## Getting to the best price

Walk the agencies on Hàng Bè in the Old Quarter and compare three quotes before booking. Prices are negotiable, especially for tours departing in 2–3 days. The base experience is nearly identical across budget operators — what you're really buying is group size and boat quality.
