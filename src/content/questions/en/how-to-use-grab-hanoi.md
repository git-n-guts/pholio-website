Grab is the most reliable transport app in Hanoi and works for visitors without any Vietnam-specific setup. Here is the complete walkthrough.

## Setup before you arrive

1. **Download Grab** from the App Store or Google Play
2. **Register** with your home phone number or email — the app works with non-Vietnamese numbers
3. **Add a payment method** — a credit card works, but set cash as the default initially (see Payment section below)
4. **Set your home country** if prompted — this doesn't affect Vietnamese fares

## Booking a ride

1. Open the app and allow location access
2. The map shows your current position — drag the pin if it's not accurate
3. **Enter your destination** in the search bar — English place names and addresses work (e.g., "Phở Thìn, Lò Đúc" or "Nội Bài Airport")
4. Select service type: **GrabBike** (motorbike, solo, cheap), **GrabCar 4** (private car, luggage ok), or **GrabCar 7** (larger groups)
5. The app shows the **price upfront** before you confirm — this is fixed unless traffic conditions change dramatically
6. Tap **Book** and wait for a driver to accept (typically 1–3 minutes in central Hanoi)

## When the driver arrives

- The app shows the driver's name, photo, license plate, and a live tracking map
- **Wait at the exact pin location** — narrow alleys can make finding you difficult; position yourself on the nearest main road if needed
- **Verify the license plate** against what the app shows before boarding
- GrabBike drivers carry a spare helmet — you're required by law to wear it

## Payment

**Cash on delivery** is the default and most reliable option. Have small bills ready — 50,000 or 100,000 VND. Drivers often don't carry change for large notes.

**Card payment** works intermittently with foreign cards. Vietnamese cards (linked to a local bank) work consistently. If you want to use a card, test it on a short low-stakes trip first.

**GrabPay wallet** — you can top up within the app using some international cards, but this varies by issuing bank.

## Common issues and fixes

| Issue | Fix |
|-------|-----|
| No drivers accepting | Switch service type; move to a main road |
| Driver calls and you can't communicate | Show them the destination on the map; point to the pin |
| App shows much higher price than expected | Wait 10–15 min for surge to pass, then rebook |
| Payment failure | Switch to cash |

## Be app — the backup

**Be** is Grab's closest Vietnamese competitor. Download it as a backup — same booking process, similar pricing, and sometimes has drivers available when Grab doesn't. Be is particularly useful in outer districts where Grab's driver density is lower.
